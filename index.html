Authorization for withholding of FICA tax in Home Help payments. Client authorizes MDHHS to act as tax-filing agent for Social Security purposes.

Medical certification completed by a physician, PA, NP, OT, PT, or CNS. Certifies patient's need for home help services with ADL/IADL and complex care selection.
