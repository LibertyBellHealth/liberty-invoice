Pre-fill and print Michigan state forms for your clients.

DHS-390 — Adult Services Application
Application for Home Help, Adult Community Placement, or other adult services. Sections for client info, program selection, current situation, and signature.
DHS-4771 — FICA Tax Authorization (Home Help)
Authorization for withholding of FICA tax in Home Help payments. Client authorizes MDHHS to act as tax-filing agent for Social Security purposes.
MDHHS-6200 — Adult Services Medical Needs Certification
Medical certification completed by a physician, PA, NP, OT, PT, or CNS. Certifies patient's need for home help services with ADL/IADL and complex care selection.
MSA-4676 — Home Help Services Agreement
Agreement between the beneficiary and caregiver/agency provider. Documents terms of Home Help service delivery, caregiver information, and start date.
BPHASA-2421 — Live-In Caregiver Attestation
Required annually for caregivers who live with the beneficiary and qualify for the EVV exemption. Signed by the caregiver, then submitted to MDHHS or an Approving Entity for countersignature.
